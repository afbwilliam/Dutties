Detailed Descriptions of DE Options

ccreform (string) Reformulation option passed to JAMS

This option determines how to formulate the indicator contraints of the chance constraints in the deterministic equivalent. The model is passed to JAMS for the actual reformulation and solution with a subsolver. The possible reformulation options are bigM [big eps threshold], chull [big eps], and indic. The indic setting only works with solver that under stand the indicator syntax in a GAMS option file.
(default = bigM)
